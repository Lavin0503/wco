About Wonder Co.

Wonder Co is a youth-led creative parent company based in Balbriggan, County Dublin, Ireland. We build fun, imaginative, and exciting small businesses powered by teamwork, passion, and big dreams.

Our mission is to create unique brands that inspire creativity — starting with our first child company, Wrist of Wonders, a colourful loom band bracelet business.
